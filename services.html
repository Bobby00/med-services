Services

Medical reviews

We provide our patients with professional nurses. They are available on a live-in basis or day scholar basis depending on the patients requirements.

Home based nursing care

We provide our patients with professional nurses. They are available on a live-in basis or day scholar basis depending on the patients requirements.

Physiotherapy

Our Physiotherapists offer home based services for bed ridden patients who require physiotherapy on a regular basis to prevent contractures and bed sores.

Nutritional therapy

Most patients require special diets to enable a speedy healing process. These reviews and feed requirements are done by our nutritionists.

Hospice-on-the-go Aide

Our well trained hospice nurses offer palliative hospice care from the comfort of your home.

Minor procedures

These include change of catheters (both suprapubic and urethral), management of chronic wounds and management of patients suffering from bedsores.

Non-pharmaceutical supplies

Our company provides supplies required on a regular basis by chronic patients who are receiving treatment from home. These inlude; BP machines, thermomethers, ripple mattresses, colostomy bags, corsets, wheel chairs etc.

Medical tourism services

We have a good network with doctors in India and we will sort all logistics for the patient and accompanying relatives in terms of hospital of choice and accomodation. Medical escorts are also available on request.

Medical camp event planning

Our team offers consultancy and logistics services during planning of medical camps.

Privacy policy

We honor the hippocratic oath and we are bound by patient doctor confidentiality. All our patient's data and test results are kept secure by Tresna. Only provider who sees the patient has access to the patient's data.

Consent

A written informed consent is required between patient/patient's next of kin before services are rendered. The patient should be briefed prior to any interventions undertaken.

Our staff are professionals trained and licensed to offer high standards of health care. We also ensure that we hire staff who are passionate about their work, honest, ethical and compassionate to our patients.
